Welcome to Restaurant

Welcome to Royal Paradise Hotel, where culinary artistry meets heartfelt hospitality.

At Royal Paradise Hotel, we believe that food is more than just nourishment—it's an experience that brings people together, creates memories, and sparks joy. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where people can enjoy delicious, thoughtfully prepared meals in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Founded by Royal Samarasignhe in 2001, our restaurant has grown from a small local eatery into a beloved destination for food lovers near and far. With a passion for culinary excellence and a dedication to quality, Royal Samarasignhe envisioned a place where every meal would be a celebration of flavor and every guest would feel like family.
